About Statdle

Need a dedicated place to archive your Wordle games? Want to see how other people performed on that tough Wordle day? Looking to show off your impressive guess distribution? Statdle is the site for you! Submit Wordles, view guess distributions globally or for specific Wordle days and users, and see your and other users' past Wordle games.

This site was built by Alexis Feng with Flask, Jinja, Bootstrap, and MongoDB for CMSC388J. The figures for Wordle statistics are generated with Matplotlib.
